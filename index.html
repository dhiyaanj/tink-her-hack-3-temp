What Are Traffic Violations?

Traffic violations are acts that break the law while driving or walking on public roads. Examples include speeding, running a red light, and illegal parking.

Types of Violations

  • Speeding
  • Running Red Lights
  • Illegal Parking
  • Driving Under the Influence
